INTRODUCTION TO COMPUTERS

We are living in an information age dependant upon digital information. Digital information is electronic information, the result of computer processing. Every type of job relies opon getting information, using it, managing it, and relaying information to others. Computers enable the efficient processing and storage of information.



The four operations of computers are :
  1. Input
  2. Processing
  3. Output
  4. Storage
